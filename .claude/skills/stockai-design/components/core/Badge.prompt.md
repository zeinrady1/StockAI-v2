Translucent-wash status chip / outlook pill in any of the six semantic tones.

```jsx
<Badge tone="green">Paper</Badge>
<Badge tone="purple">Opus 4.8</Badge>
<Badge tone="green" shape="pill">Bullish</Badge>
<Badge tone="red" shape="pill">Bearish</Badge>
<Badge tone="muted" shape="pill">Neutral</Badge>
```

`shape="chip"` (default) is the squared tag; `shape="pill"` is the rounded uppercase outlook label.
